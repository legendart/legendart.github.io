MemeMemo - Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

MemeMemo ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Locally stored data: All your memos, clips, and folders are stored locally on your device using SwiftData. We do not collect or access this data.

iCloud sync (optional): If you enable iCloud sync or sign in with Apple ID or Google, your data may be synced to your iCloud account or Firebase (Google Cloud). This data is only accessible to you.

Authentication data: If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive basic profile information (name, email) solely to identify your account for sync purposes. We do not share this information with third parties.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide app functionality (storing and syncing your memos)
  • To verify your in-app purchase status
  • To improve the app experience

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on your device and, if sync is enabled, in your iCloud or Firebase account. We use industry-standard security practices. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

MemeMemo uses the following third-party services when you choose to sign in:

  • Firebase (Google): Used for optional cloud sync. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Apple Sign In: Used for optional authentication. Apple Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

MemeMemo does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is rated 4+ and does not contain content inappropriate for children.

Your Rights

You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by signing out and deleting the app. To request data deletion, contact us at the email below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
